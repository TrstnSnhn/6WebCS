About the Internet

The Internet is the world's largest network that connects millions of computers and device across the globe. it allows people to communicate,access information, conduct business, and enjoy entertainment. Today the internet plays an important role in education, communication and everyday life.

My 5 Key Learnings

  • The Internet is a global network connecting billions of devices
  • HTML is the standard language use to create webpages.
  • Websites are collection of related webpages.
  • HTTP is the protocol used to transfer webpages over the internet
  • Website planning is important for creating user friendly websites

Internet Terminology

Term Description

World Wide Web (WWW) Collection of Webpages accessible through the internet
