# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Recruiters, potential collaborators, and peers interested in the personal and professional background of Angelo Tristan D. Sinohin.

## Product Purpose
A personal profile page designed to showcase the user's academic background (BSCS), hobbies, skills, and career aspirations in a modern, professional, and engaging format.

## Brand Personality
Professional, Minimalist, Creative, Approachable.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, generic corporate templates.
- Overly bright, uncoordinated color schemes.
- Static, lifeless layouts without micro-interactions.

## Design Principles
- **Visual Clarity:** Information should be easy to scan and digest.
- **Minimalist Elegance:** High-quality typography and intentional white space over decorative elements.
- **Professional Craft:** Attention to micro-interactions and smooth transitions to show technical attention to detail.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance for color contrast.
- Respect for `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- Semantic HTML for screen readers.
